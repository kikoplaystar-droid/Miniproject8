About Sushi‑Manga

Where Japanese cuisine meets manga culture

Our Story

Sushi‑Manga was created with one idea in mind — to combine the authentic taste of Japanese cuisine with the cozy and creative atmosphere of manga cafés. We believe food should not only taste great but also inspire.

Our chefs prepare every dish using fresh, high‑quality ingredients, while our interior and concept bring the world of manga to life. Whether you're here for a quick lunch, a relaxing dinner, or to enjoy your favorite manga — Sushi‑Manga is the perfect place.
